Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

VirtualService ("we", "our", "the platform") is a web application that allows registered users to create, configure and manage mock REST web services for development and testing purposes. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

2. Data We Collect

Account data

  • Email address — used to identify your account and, for local registrations, to send a verification email.
  • Password (local accounts only) — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash; we never store it in plain text.
  • Google account ID and profile picture — collected only when you choose to sign in with Google OAuth. The profile picture URL is stored to display your avatar in the application.

Service data

  • Mock service definitions, API call configurations, response templates and test databases you create through the editor.
  • Request logs generated when your mock services receive HTTP calls (stored temporarily for the real-time monitor feature).

Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect payment information, precise geolocation, browsing history outside our platform, or any data beyond what is strictly necessary to operate the service.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session via JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • Provide you with a private workspace isolated from other users.
  • Store and serve the mock service configurations you create.
  • Display your avatar and email in the application toolbar when you are logged in.
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password reset) when you register with a local account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data may be disclosed only if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of the platform and its users.

5. Third-Party Services

When you choose to authenticate with Google Sign-In, authentication is handled by Google LLC under their Privacy Policy. We only receive the data Google provides after your explicit consent (email, Google ID, profile picture).

6. Data Retention

Your account data and service configurations are retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account at any time from the account management section; all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days of the request.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for Google OAuth by revoking access in your Google Account settings.

8. Security

We implement standard security measures including HTTPS transport encryption, bcrypt password hashing and JWT-based session management. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at: virtual.service.leo@gmail.com
